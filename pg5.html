STEP 4

Rules and Precautions

01. ALL SORTS OF SAMPLES(SOIL, FOOD, ROCKS, ETC.) ARE PROHIBITED FROM BEING CARRIED TO OR FROM EARTH.
STRICT ACTION WILL BE TAKEN AGAINST THOSE WHO DISOBEY.

02. Medical check ups are mandatory. Due to differences in gravitational force, loss of height
or bone problems may arise during your stay on Earth.

03. The atmosphere on Earth is rich in oxygen and nitrogen in comparison to Mars
where the atmospheric compostion is mainly carbon dioxide.
