BRIEF OVERLOOK INTO EARTH'S HISTORY

Earth was formed around the same time as our planet Mars- nearly 4.6 billion years ago- and is said to be a mixture of dust and gas. It is twice as large as Mars and considered very similar in terms of basic composition and structure- especially since they are both terrestrial planets. However, unlike Mars, which only contains water in the form of ice, it is composed of seventy percent liquid water.
